BOLLYWOOD MUSIC

Bollywood songs, more formally known as Hindi movie songs, are songs featured in Bollywood films, often performed in item numbers. The language of Hindi movie songs, generally termed Hindi, can be complex. Some songs are saturated with Urdu and Persian terms and it is not uncommon to hear use of English words in songs from modern Hindi movies. Several other Indian languages have also been used including Braj, Bhojpuri, Punjabi and Rajasthani. Occasionally a few lines in other Indian languages are used as well.
